Previous Projects

Comb-Slime

Unity

C#

Game Jam

This game was made during a game jam with the theme "Better Together". In this game you play as 2 slimes who need to come together to solve puzzles. They can combine to create a big slime that can push heavy objects and split apart gaining massive speed in the desired direction to clear obstacles.

The Squares

Unity

C#

A 2D minimalist action game I worked on during one summer. In this game you play as a circle getting his revenge on the squares. You have one rectangular gun which has multiple firing modes and can be changed into a shield to block bullets and regain ammo.

Trigger Happy

Unity

C#

This game was made during a game jam with the theme "Out Of Control" In this FPS you must shoot all the blocks before the ammo in your gun runs out.

Cave Divers

Unity

C#

My Final year project focusing on precedural level generation. Cave Divers has you explore procedurally generated caves where you will find a plethora of treasure and enemies to engage with.

Brick Shaker

GDScript

Mobile Dev

My first published mobile game. Brick Shaker is a twist on the classic game Brick Breaker where instead of hitting the ball with a paddle to break the bricks you play as the bricks and must tilt your phone side to side to avoid the ball.
